# Who am I?

As an engineer, I hail from the annals of history when the internet was dial-up and the modem actually made sounds. Before any of you call me a boomer, please remember, that my callouses tell the tales of broken commits and rebased branches long before Git was cool (someone bring up SVN, please). And I will allow everyone to judge me once they see what my wallpaper looked like in 2011.

# What I'm looking for

To be honest, I want to be stimulated academically and want to solve problems. It's the whole reason I chose to go into engineering. People often ask me how I manage to change fields so many times. I just tend to point out, that at the core of all those experiences is an innate curiosity of mine to solve the problem at hand. Whether it be upper-limb prosthetics, writing articles for a local magazine, designing VLSI chips or more recently working on Quantum Photonics. They have all inculcated some or the other skill in me and I have learnt something new from each experience. THAT is invaluable.

To sum it up... I'M LOOKING FOR CHALLENGES.
